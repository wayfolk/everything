Bringing Earth to Life.
All Over Again.

Born out of B-Reel stateside, the idea of taking Google Earth’s rich dataset and building a user-friendly video-editing-tool out of it, felt like sheer genius. If a tad daunting.

Luckily the Google Geo team took notice of the early experiments and expanded the project into a full-blown product collab. The output of which you can see reflected daily on the world’s biggest websites and tv channels.

What’s that? Oh yea. It’s delivered as a highly optimised wasm runtime, right in your browser. Chrome magic.
