The World’s Most Exciting Brief.
That Wasn’t One.

2-D, Russel, Noodle and Murdoc dropped off the map. Where did they go? What were they up to?! Who can say.
All we know a most peculiar house popped up, and rumours are afoot. Could be it be? Could Gorillaz be back, after what feels like a g-damn eternity?

The crew at B-Reel heard whispers on the wind, and after weeks seances the tealeaves could no longer be ignored. Gorillaz were on their way back!

We approached the band and humbly asked if they would lend our skills to deliver something truly special to accompany their latest musical masterpiece.

The end result was a mindblowing mobile app that explored their new housings in AR, VR and whateverelseR you liked. Up to, and including a full livestream of the entire album upon release. For free. Truly bonkers innit?
